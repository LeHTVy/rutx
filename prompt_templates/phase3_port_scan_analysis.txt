## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

### 1. Service Identification
- Extract all open ports and services
- Identify service versions
- Flag outdated/EOL software

### 2. Vulnerability Correlation
- Cross-reference service versions with CVE databases
- Check Shodan for known exposures
- Identify OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities

### 3. Risk Assessment
- Categorize findings: CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
- Assess exploitability (public exploits available?)
- Evaluate potential business impact

### 4. Evidence Collection
- Document exact commands that produced findings
- Include raw tool output as proof
- Provide reproducible steps

### 5. Recommendations
- Prioritize by actual risk (not theoretical)
- Provide specific remediation steps
- Include verification commands

---
